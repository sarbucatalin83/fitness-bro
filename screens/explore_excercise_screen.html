Overview

The bench press is a classic compound exercise that primarily targets the pectoralis major, anterior deltoids, and triceps. It is fundamental for building upper body pushing strength and muscle mass.

Tips for Proper Form

  • check
    Plant your feet Keep your feet flat on the floor to generate power and maintain stability throughout the lift.
  • check
    Control the descent Lower the bar slowly to your mid-chest, avoiding bouncing it off your ribcage.
  • check
